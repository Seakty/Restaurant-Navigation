About Our Project

A platform connecting food lovers to top restaurants and gathering authentic reviews.

Our Mission

Our goal is to create an intuitive, user-friendly platform that allows users to discover restaurants, share honest feedback, and help businesses improve their services. By focusing on user experience and data accuracy, we aim to revolutionize how people connect with culinary destinations.

What This Website Offers

This website provides a seamless experience for browsing restaurants, leaving reviews, and reading feedback from other users. Each restaurant profile contains detailed information, ratings, and real customer experiences, helping users make informed dining choices.

Why We Built This

This project is a collaborative effort from students passionate about web development and data science. It was built as part of our OOP project to demonstrate our skills in creating modern web applications that solve real-world problems by connecting businesses and customers.
